  • Petra Janney

    Co-Founder
    Los Angeles

  • Petra has built a career helping large organizations envision and create better futures. Before Hatcher, Petra led creative development at a boutique prototyping firm, where she collaborated with companies to develop new products, generate new business areas, and reimagine their vision and trajectory in a way that speaks to emerging markets. Spanning from sustainable design to flight systems, space operations, green technology, educational experiences, data visualization, consumer product innovation, and mobile laboratories, Petra has worked in multidisciplinary teams with a wide range of Fortune 500 companies, large government organizations, and six branches of the US military.

    Petra graduated Magna Cum Laude from Harvard University with a degree in History & Science and a secondary concentration in Global Health - but her strongest focus has always been on the future. In her own work, she has investigated the environmental and healthcare applications of 3D printing, tested concepts for recyclable photovoltaic cells, and won competitions for product and experience design. Petra is also a pilot, and in her free time she flies shelter pets off death row and into new homes through her nonprofit, Amelia Air.

  • Peter Presley

    Co-Founder
    San Francisco

  • Peter brings 20+ years of management consulting, strategic innovation, and design firm experience, with the majority of his career spent at Deloitte and frog. With an outstanding record of impact, including selling and delivering $60m+ of hardware, service and digital innovation to Disney, Peter has led a significant number of transformational projects for Airbus, Intel, AT&T, Gillette, Unilever, Toyota, Coca Cola, GE, UPS, DoCoMo, Sony and more. Peter thrives on being a business-trusted advisor and has worked on a broad array of projects including business model innovation, strategic sourcing, partnerships, product development, PMO, IT planning/system selections, customer development, channel strategy, market-entry, sales force effectiveness and business operational reviews.

    Originally from the UK, Peter has extensive global experience working across Europe, the US, Asia-Japan, and Latin America. This work experience has generated an impressive global network of capabilities (e.g. talent, full service providers, boutiques, clients, sponsors, and investors). Since 2005, Peter has been based in the Silicon Valley area and is well connected within the ecosystem. Peter continues to start and scale new entrepreneurial efforts collaborating with high caliber multidisciplinary teams, work that includes current Hatcher and MadeIn ventures.

  • Mike Keesling

    Co-Founder
    Los Angeles

  • Mike was mentored by special effects designers, toy inventors and Walt Disney engineers in the fields of electronics and mechanics. By the time he was in his early teens, Mike was working full time, designing and manufacturing mechanical components and electronics systems for remote camera cranes in the motion picture industry. He used the money he earned to buy computers, electronics, lasers and synthesizers so he could keep building the creations he imagined.

    Mike has worked most of his life in the motion picture and R&D industries, where he jumps between precise micro-miniature optics and non-precious prototypes with ease. He does this by leveraging his skills in electronics, mechanics, programming, optics, and robotics. He is equally comfortable rapid-prototyping ideas in hours as he is spending multiple years on projects with dozens of team members. Mike is an accomplished inventor with several patents in the fields of optics, sensing, motion control and interfaces. In addition, Mike has an Academy Award and an Emmy for his technical contributions to the television and cinematic arts.
